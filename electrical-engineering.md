# Electrical Engineering Cheatsheet

## Ohm's law

V = I R

Voltage (v) = Current (amp) * Resistance (Ω)


## How to measure

1. Voltage: in circuit
1. Current (ampere): NOT in circuit
1. Resistance (ohm): NOT in circuit


## Measurements

- giga  -- 1GA = 1kkk A
- mega  -- 1MA = 1kk A
- kilo  -- 1kA = 1k A
- milli -- 1mA = 1A/1k
- micro -- 1μA = 1A/1kk
- nano  -- 1nA = 1A/1kkk
- pico  -- 1pA = 1A/1kkkk
